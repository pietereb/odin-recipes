Vegetable wok

Can AI cook well? ChatGPT came up with this recipe, and I must admit: the taste is truly good.

The default recipe is not vegetarian/vegan, but can be adapted really easily to fit a more plant-based lifestyle.

Ingredients

  • Anchovies (vegan alternative: miso paste)
  • Sesame oil
  • Garlic
  • Chili flakes
  • Carrot
  • Broccoli
  • Shiitake
  • Bell pepper (red)
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce (vegan alternative: black bean sauce)
  • Honey (alternative: sugar)
  • Rice vinegar
  • Taugeh
  • Spring onions
  • Rice or noodles

Steps

  1. Boil the rice or noodles.
  2. Bake the anchovies, garlic and chili flakes in sesame oil.
  3. Add the carrots, broccoli, shiitake and bell pepper. Bake over high heat.
  4. For the sauce, mix the soy sauce, oyster sauce, honey and rice vinegar. Add the sauce to the vegetables as soon as they are tender.
  5. Simmer in the sauce for few minutes, add the taugeh and spring rolls just one minute before serving.
  6. Serve with rice or noodles.
