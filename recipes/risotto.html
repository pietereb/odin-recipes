Risotto

Although not authentically Italian, this assembled recipe tastes delicious. Try it out for yourself!

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Peas
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Arborio rice
  • Broth
  • White wine
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Sage
  • Almonds

Steps

  1. Warm the oven to 200 C, set to fan.
  2. Mix the cherry tomatoes with olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper and sage. Bake in the oven for 30-40 minutes until the tomatoes are well baked.
  3. Meanwhile, caramelize the onions and garlic.
  4. Add the carrots and rice, bake shortly and deglace with white wine.
  5. Keep adding the broth in batches until the rice is cooked and keep the fire on a simmer.
  6. Brown the almonds in a pan.
  7. Add the peas to the risotto and stir them through the risotto.
  8. Place the oven-baked tomates on top and finish with the almonds and grated parmesan cheese.
